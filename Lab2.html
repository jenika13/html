Škoda Slavia 1.5 TSI launched at Rs 16.19 lakh

Shortly after announcing prices for Slavia 1.0 TSI, Škoda has now launched the performance oriented 1.5 TSI in India.

After initially introducing the sedan with a sole 1.0-litre TSI motor, Skoda has now finally launched the 1.5-litre TSI engine for Slavia. The bigger engine is available exclusively on the range-topping Style trim, with the manual variant being proced at Rs.16.19 lakh, while the automatic version costs 17.79 lakh (both prices, ex-showroom). "The SLAVIA 1.5 TSI follows in the spirit and legacy of high-performance SKODAs from the past, and is thereason we have retained the option of a manual gearbox, along with the DSG automatic. We are confident our customers will experience and appreciate the distinction from the 1.0 TSI and enjoy the tangible and intangible embellishments that the 1.5 TSI offers."-says Zac Hollis,Brand Director, Škoda Auto India.

The highlight of the Skoda Slavia 1.5 TSI is ofcourse the larger engine. The four-cylinder 1.5-litre turbo-petrol unit makes 150bhp and 250Nm of torque. This unit comes paired to either a 6-speed manual or a 7speed DSG transmission. Škoda claims that the car can accelerate to a hundred in 8.8 seconds and a fuel efficiency of 18.72 kmpl. The engine also gets Active Cylinder Technology, shutting down two of the cylinders if driven in low load, maximising efficiency, this tech is often seen in cars much pricier.
